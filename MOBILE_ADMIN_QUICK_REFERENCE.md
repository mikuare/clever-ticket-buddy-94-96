## â“ FAQ

**Q: Why don't I see "Assign to Me" anymore?**  
A: The ticket is already assigned. Check the badge to see who has it.

**Q: Why can't I resolve a ticket I'm viewing?**  
A: You can only resolve tickets assigned to **you**. Check for the blue "Assigned to You" badge.

**Q: I assigned a ticket by accident, can I unassign?**  
A: Use the "Refer to Other Admin" feature (desktop) or ask admin to reassign.

**Q: What does the "New" badge mean?**  
A: The user has sent new messages since you last checked the chat.

**Q: Can I see all my assigned tickets?**  
A: Use the filter options in the Tickets tab to filter by your assignments.
